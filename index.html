As a person who has spent the last half year intensely studying and learning the different aspects of web development, I have not only seen the limitless potential of creativity, but also the potential advantages the industry can offer to just about anyone. Most of the time, when we search for something simple, we get something simple. Be it a webpage that simply displays information or redirects you to some other site. I believe that this is a waste of what a website can do. With the ability to send and summon information and render it in a large variety of ways, there is no excuse to have a website created with next to no functionality. Having come from a science and political background, inefficiencies and lack of function has always been a pet peeve of mine and I often find myself constantly looking for ways to improve any project I have had the privilege of participating in.
